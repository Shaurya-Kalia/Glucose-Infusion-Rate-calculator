Important: This is a program to calculate volumes of fluids to be given to neonates for specified rates of GIR. Made for a particular setting for my hospital and may not be widely deployable. This tool is for educational/reference purposes, and does not replace clinical judgement. I assume no liability for any clinical outcome.

Assumptions:

  1. Complete fluid requirement is to be fulfilled via IV route.
  2. Potassium required is 1mEq/kg/day after day 2.
  3. Calcium is not added.
  4. Sodium correction (N/5) after day 2.
  5. Fluid requirements are taken as per the table:
Day of Life Term (mL/kg/day) Preterm (mL/kg/day)

Limitations:

  1. Doesn't account for oral feeds.
  2. Small volumes of KCl (<0.5 ml) are ignored in 60 ml syringe formulation (Multiply manually as necessary from the KCl value in the hourly table).
  3. Intentionally limits GIR to 4 - 12 mg/kg/min.
  4. Not applicable to situations where the glucose concentration of fluid is <50mg/ml or >250mg/ml.
